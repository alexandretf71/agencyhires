Questions

What is Volley and how is it different?

Volley is a language learning app paired with a real human tutor. Between lessons, you practice daily with an AI conversation partner, personalized audio lessons, and smart flashcards. What makes it different is the loop: your tutor reviews your actual practice data each week and adjusts your plan — so you're always working on the right thing, not just following a generic course.

How much does Volley cost?

Volley is $99/month. That includes 4 × 30-minute live lessons with a real tutor each month, plus unlimited AI practice in the app every day. Private tutors alone run $200+/month for fewer sessions with no integrated practice between them. You can cancel anytime — no contracts.

Why is this better than Duolingo or Babbel?

Apps are great for building habits. They're not great at noticing when you're plateauing, avoiding certain grammar, or skipping days without consequence. A real tutor does all three. Volley pairs both: the AI keeps you consistent daily; the tutor keeps you honest and progressing weekly.

Is the tutor a real person?

Yes. A real, qualified language tutor — not an AI acting as one. They review your app practice data every week and run your 30-minute live sessions with you.

What languages do you offer?

Take the 2-minute placement test to see the languages currently available for your level. Volley is expanding its tutor network regularly.

How much time does it take each day?

15 minutes is enough to make real progress. The app is designed for commutes, lunch breaks, and dead time — not 2-hour study blocks. Your tutor sets the weekly plan so you're never guessing how to fill the time.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, anytime — no contracts, no cancellation fees. Most people don't because the weekly tutor check-in is what actually keeps them coming back.
