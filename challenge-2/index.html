The Hunt

How I
searched.

I avoided generic VP Sales / CRO profiles and looked for people already operating near Snowball's real commercial edge: YouTube-native content, creator partnerships, brand integrations, video IP monetization and creator-led communities.

The bet is that Snowball's Head of Sales should be able to sell YouTube as a strategic growth channel — not just sell agency services. That's why I prioritized people from Good Story Studios, Moonbug and Tribe Gaming rather than obvious agency sales executives. Each one is already doing the kind of commercial work Snowball needs, and none of them are sitting on a recruiter's list.

Shortlist
3 candidates

Candidate 01

Barbara Abraul

Brand Partnerships Good Story Studios
in LinkedIn ↗

Why she'd crush it

Barbara is a very strong non-obvious pick because she is already doing the kind of commercial work Snowball needs: helping brands partner with serious YouTube-native creators. Good Story Studios describes itself as a Venice-based company helping brands do YouTube the right way — turning brand campaigns into cultural moments through trusted internet storytellers.

She is not a generic sales executive. She is already operating inside the exact market Snowball is trying to win.

The outreach DM

LinkedIn · New Message
Barbara — I came across your work with Good Story Studios and Theorist Media, and it immediately felt relevant to Snowball. They're building a YouTube-first agency for brands, and the role is less about traditional sales and more about helping brands understand how to win on YouTube with the right creative and creator strategy. Your work around brand partnerships for top YouTube creators feels very close to that problem. Would you be open to a quick conversation?
